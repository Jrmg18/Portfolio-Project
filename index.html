About Me

From saving hearts to crafting digital experiences

I'm transitioning from a career as a Surgical Care Practitioner in Cardiac Surgery, Urology and Thoracic Robotics Surgery to frontend development. After years of precision work in high-stakes environments, I discovered my passion for creating beautiful, functional web experiences.

My medical background taught me the importance of attention to detail, problem-solving under pressure, and continuous learning - skills that translate perfectly to writing clean, reliable code. Currently studying through Codecademy, I'm excited to bring my dedication and systematic approach to the world of web development.

I believe in writing clean, maintainable code and staying up-to-date with the latest web development trends. When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring new technologies or working on personal projects.
